# Heroes of the Storm Knowledge Base

## Maps and Strategies

### Alterac Pass
- **Strengths**: teamfight, brawlers, sustain
- **Strategy**: Extended teamfights around cavalry objectives. Brawlers and sustain compositions excel. Strong solo lane on top is important.
- **Key heroes**: Sonya, Thrall, Rehgar, E.T.C.

### Battlefield of Eternity
- **Strengths**: burst damage, sustain healers, single target
- **Strategy**: Race to burn down the Immortal. Burst damage and sustain healers are key. AA heroes with sustain DPS shine.
- **Key heroes**: Valla, Greymane, Raynor, Li-Ming

### Blackheart's Bay
- **Strengths**: split push, waveclear, globals
- **Strategy**: Coin collection rewards split push and waveclear. Globals like Falstad and Dehaka can rotate quickly.
- **Key heroes**: Falstad, Dehaka, Zagara, Sylvanas

### Braxis Holdout
- **Strengths**: summons, solo lane, waveclear
- **Strategy**: Dual-lane control. Strong solo laners dominate top. Zagara and Abathur are particularly strong.
- **Key heroes**: Zagara, Sonya, Rexxar, Arthas

### Cursed Hollow
- **Strengths**: sustain, CC, rotation
- **Strategy**: Large map with roaming tributes. Sustain and heavy CC compositions thrive. Global heroes add macro value.
- **Key heroes**: Falstad, Dehaka, Brightwing, E.T.C.

### Dragon Shire
- **Strengths**: dive, mobility, dual soak
- **Strategy**: Dual shrine control demands mobility. Dive compositions are highly valued. Need strong solo lane.
- **Key heroes**: Tyrael, Dehaka, Illidan, Sonya

### Garden of Terror
- **Strengths**: poke, siege, sustain
- **Strategy**: Three-lane map with seed objectives. Poke and siege damage push structures effectively.
- **Key heroes**: Sylvanas, Ragnaros, Falstad, Jaina

### Hanamura Temple
- **Strengths**: poke, sustain, AoE
- **Strategy**: Payload escort map. Poke, sustain, and AoE compositions dominate the narrow lanes.
- **Key heroes**: Hanzo, Fenix, Lúcio, Johanna

### Infernal Shrines
- **Strengths**: burst AoE, teamfight, waveclear
- **Strategy**: Kill 40 guardians fast. Burst AoE and strong teamfighting are crucial.
- **Key heroes**: Jaina, Kael'thas, Johanna, Maiev, Xul

### Sky Temple
- **Strengths**: poke, long range, control
- **Strategy**: Temple control zones favor poke compositions and long-range heroes.
- **Key heroes**: Chromie, Hanzo, Li-Ming, Fenix

### Tomb of the Spider Queen
- **Strengths**: waveclear, split push, sustain
- **Strategy**: Small map focused on gem collection. Waveclear and split push specialists thrive.
- **Key heroes**: Zagara, Xul, Nazeebo, Sylvanas

### Towers of Doom
- **Strengths**: sustain, control, teamfight
- **Strategy**: Core cannot be attacked directly. Altar control deals core damage. Sustain and teamfight compositions excel. Sappers from forts add pressure.
- **Key heroes**: E.T.C., Johanna, Malfurion, Sylvanas

### Volskaya Foundry
- **Strengths**: CC, teamfight, AoE
- **Strategy**: Conveyors add positioning dynamics. CC-heavy teamfight compositions are ideal.
- **Key heroes**: E.T.C., Garrosh, Jaina, Zarya

### Warhead Junction
- **Strengths**: poke, structures, globals
- **Strategy**: Large map with scattered nukes. Poke, siege, and global heroes dominate.
- **Key heroes**: Falstad, Dehaka, Abathur, Sgt. Hammer

## Roles

| Role | Description | Key Responsibility |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| Tank | Main frontline, initiation, peel | Engage/disengage, protect backline |
| Bruiser | Off-tank, solo lane, sustained damage | Solo lane, secondary frontline |
| Healer | Main healing output | Keep team alive, enable trades |
| Support | Utility, shields, globals | Map control, enabling plays |
| Melee Assassin | Close-range burst/sustained damage | Kill priority targets, dive |
| Ranged Assassin | Long-range damage output | Sustained/burst damage from safety |

## Draft Mechanics

### Standard Draft Order
1. Ban Phase 1 (2 bans each, 4 total)
2. Pick Phase 1 (picks alternate)
3. Ban Phase 2 (1 ban each, 2 total)
4. Pick Phase 2 (remaining picks)

### Key Principles
- **1-3-1 vs 2-2-1**: Map-dependent rotation strategy
  - 1-3-1: Strong solo laners on each side lane (Dragon Shire, Braxis)
  - 2-2-1: Two pairs in lanes with roaming (Cursed Hollow, Sky Temple)
- **Double Support**: Viable when team has sustained damage (Valla, Raynor)
- **Dive Composition**: Needs a dive tank (Anub'arak, Tyrael, Diablo) + dive damage
- **CC Rule**: No more than 2 heroes without CC in a composition
- **Counter-pick vs Synergize**: Counter-pick when enemy has a dominant threat; synergize when building around a strategy
